BUSKING IN THE DARK

Busking in the Dark is a short documentary about visually impaired busker, Daniel Ng who struggles to pursue his passion for music. Through this film, we aim to spread awareness about busking in Singapore and how it is too, a respectable job.

Role: Camera Operator

OLD HABITS DIE HARD

Old Habits Die Hard is a documentary done solely by myself in Year 2.

Old Habits Die Hard features Don - café owner and an avid antique collector. Together with his wife, Selena, they travel across the globe in search of antiques. With the hopes of bringing the three generations together, and allowing the newer generation to experience what it was like in the past, Don displays his collections up in the café for people to admire and purchase.

Role: Director of Photography and Scriptwriter
