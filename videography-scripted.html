LOST & FOUND

Lost & Found is an original student production done by my group and I for one of our projects.

It is a meet-cute story on how Jasmine, a morally upright girl, who found a ten dollar note on the floor and is determined to give the money back to its rightful owner. After two days of searching, Jasmine eventually gives up, and that's when she meets Wei Lun.
